ResearchGate

Research Opportunity Platform

A mobile app that acts as a central hub for researchers to create events and for students to discover and join research opportunities — bridging a 73% awareness gap.

Problem

Students — especially high schoolers — often lack awareness about research opportunities, creating an information gap between them and the research community. Researchers also struggle to reach younger audiences efficiently.

Goal

Create a service that spreads awareness about research opportunities and bridges the information gap between students and researchers at every level.

Stakeholders
Researchers Institutions High School Students College Students
Research Findings
  • Only 29% of Gen Z students cite STEM careers as their first choice despite 75% expressing interest in STEM fields.
  • College research creates the foundation for major advances in health, medicine, communication, and national security.
  • Adolescents ask insightful questions that differ from those of advanced researchers — valuable input often untapped.
  • Universities can help keep STEM education engaging for younger students with early exposure.
Research Methodology
Heuristic Evaluation Interviews Secondary Research GenderMag Walkthroughs
Key Insights
  • Researchers are motivated but lack time to organize outreach events.
  • Researchers see strong potential in highly motivated college students.
  • AI should act as a supplemental tool rather than replacing human interaction.
  • Students need greater exposure to research opportunities early on.
Features
  • Research event discovery with personalized recommendations
  • AI-assisted event creation to reduce researcher effort
  • Lab and researcher follow system for ongoing engagement
  • Completion badges and engagement tracking for students
Design Solutions by Barrier
Low Student Motivation
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Completion badges
  • Skill-building opportunities
Researcher Time Constraints
  • AI event creation helper
  • Low-effort event formats
  • Research presentation opportunities
Student Immaturity
  • Interest screening questions
  • Follow-up involvement encouragement
ROI for Institutions
  • Follow-up surveys
  • Lab following features
  • Long-term retention tracking
