ResearchGate

Research Opportunity Platform

A mobile app that acts as a central hub for researchers to create events and opportunities for students to discover and join.

Problem

Students, especially high school students, often lack awareness about research opportunities, creating an information gap between them and the research community. Researchers also struggle to reach younger audiences.

Goal

Create a service that spreads awareness about research opportunities and bridges the information gap between students and researchers.

Stakeholders

  • Researchers
  • Institutions
  • High school students
  • College students

Research findings

  • College research creates the foundation for major advances in health, medicine, communication, economics, energy, and national security.
  • Adolescents ask insightful questions that differ from those of advanced researchers.
  • Universities can help keep STEM education engaging for younger students.
  • Only 29% of Gen Z students cite STEM careers as their first choice despite 75% expressing interest in STEM fields.

Methodology

  • Heuristic evaluation
  • Interviews
  • Secondary research
  • GenderMag walkthroughs

Key insights

  • Researchers are motivated but lack time to organize outreach events.
  • Researchers see strong potential in highly motivated college students.
  • AI should act as a supplemental tool rather than replacing human interaction.
  • Students need greater exposure to research opportunities early on.

Features

  • Research event discovery
  • Personalized recommendations
  • AI-assisted event creation
  • Lab and researcher follow system
  • Completion badges and engagement tracking

Product requirements

Student incentives

  • College and career preparation
  • Early exposure to research
  • Networking opportunities
  • Research skill development

Researcher incentives

  • Training future researchers
  • Increased student involvement
  • Long-term return on investment
  • Improved college retention

Limitations addressed

Low student motivation

  • Personalized recommendations
  • Completion badges
  • Skill-building opportunities

Researcher motivation

  • AI event creation helper
  • Low-effort event formats
  • Research presentation opportunities

Immaturity

  • Interest screening questions
  • Follow-up involvement encouragement

ROI

  • Follow-up surveys
  • Encouragement to join future events
  • Lab following features

Metrics

Awareness Gap

73%

73% of surveyed college students reported they were unaware of research opportunities in high school

Stakeholder Interviews

5

Identified 4 core issues: awareness, resources, platform, and time
