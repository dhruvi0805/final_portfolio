Plant Doctor App

A mobile app that diagnoses plant diseases via real-time scans and provides comprehensive care tracking — designed for plant owners who struggle with species-specific care.

Problem

Many plant owners struggle with keeping their plants alive due to a lack of awareness of specific care requirements for different species or misdiagnosed issues — leading to frustration, wasted time, and low satisfaction.

Goal

Create a solution to reduce diagnosis time and improve user adherence to a plant care schedule through intelligent guidance and reminders.

Research Findings
  • 24% of Americans admit they cannot keep a houseplant alive for more than six months.
  • The average plant parent has killed seven indoor plants.
  • Overwatering, underwatering, neglect, and over-fertilizing are the top four causes of plant death.
  • One-third of new plant owners struggle specifically with watering frequency.
Research Methodology
Interviews Contextual Inquiries Secondary Research
Key Insights
  • People often forget to tend to their plants due to busy lifestyles.
  • Plant owners lack knowledge about species-specific care requirements.
  • Users do not always have the time or energy to research plant care independently.
Features
  • Adding and organizing plants with species-specific care profiles
  • Weekly progress tracking and care history
  • Personalized motivational messages and care reminders
  • Best care environment recommendations per species
  • AI-assisted diagnosis and treatment recommendations linked to a storefront
