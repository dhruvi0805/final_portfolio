Thrive

Plant Doctor App

A mobile app that diagnoses plant diseases via real-time scans and provides comprehensive care tracking.

Problem

Many plant owners struggle with keeping their plants alive due to a lack of awareness of specific care requirements for different species or misdiagnosed issues. This often leads to frustration, wasted time, and a low satisfaction rate.

Goal

Create a solution to reduce diagnosis time and improve user adherence to a plant care schedule.

Stakeholders

  • Plant owners

Research findings

  • 24% of Americans admit they cannot keep a houseplant alive for more than six months.
  • The average plant parent has killed seven indoor plants.
  • Overwatering, underwatering, neglect, and over-fertilizing are the top four causes of plant death.
  • One-third of new plant owners struggle with watering.

Methodology

  • Interviews
  • Contextual inquiries
  • Secondary research

Key insights

  • People often forget to tend to their plants due to busy lifestyles.
  • Plant owners lack knowledge about species-specific care requirements.
  • Users do not always have the time or energy to research plant care.

Features

  • Adding and organizing plants
  • Weekly progress tracking
  • Personalized motivational messages
  • Best care environment recommendations
  • Diagnosis and treatment recommendations linked to a storefront

Key screens

Plant Profile

Plant summary and care history

To-Do List

Track watering, fertilizing, and care tasks

Plant Diagnosis

Identify diseases and issues through scanning

Plant Shop

Storefront for plant care products

Explore

Tips and tricks for plant care
